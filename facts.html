FACTS

Patagonia refers to a geographical regionthat encompasses the southern end of South America, governed by Argentina and Chile.


region comprises the southern section of Andes with lakes, fjords, rain forests and glaciers In the West and deserts, tablelands and steppes to the East.


Patagonia is bounded by Pacific Ocean and Atlantic Ocean. There is also strait of Magellan, the Beagle Channel and the Drake Passage to the South.
