PLANNING A TRIP

There are multiple ways to get to Patagonia, from all over the world. The closest you will get by plane is to get a flight that arrives to Ushuaia International airport.


From there it is very easy to navigate around Patagonia region. There are also very good public transportation options by bus and you will also get there by boat or by car if you wish.
